III3rd Legion Emperor's Children

Quick Facts

  • Name: IIIrd Legion Emperor's Children
  • Primarch: Fulgrim
  • Bodyguard: The Phoenix Guard
  • Homeworld: Chemos
  • Flagship: Pride of the Emperor
  • Organisation: Millenials > Cohorts > Companies
  • Strength: 110,000

History

The Emperor's Children were initially employed as equerries and bodyguards to officials. They also led Imperial armies into battle

Observations

The Emperor's Children favour speed and mobility in battle, and are excellent at combined-arms warfare

  • Homeworld: Nostramo
  • Flagship: Nightfall
  • Organisation: Chapters > Companies

History

The Night Lords favour terror tactics, often making bloody examples of their enemies in order to undermine morale
