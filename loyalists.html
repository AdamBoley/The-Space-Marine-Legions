The Loyalist Legions

Those Legions who remained loyal to the Emperor of Mankind, the Nine Who Stood, were were able to prevail against their traitorous kin through tenacity and skill at arms. They were unique military organisations, each with their own combat doctrines, rich histories and specialised units, which combined to see them win the Horus Heresy. See below for a deatiled breakdown of these famous, hard-fighting organisations
